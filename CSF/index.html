School Founder
Daniele Comboni
was an Italian Roman Catholic bishop who served in the missions in Africa and was the founder of both the Comboni Missionaries of the Heart of Jesus and the Comboni Missionary Sisters. Born: March 15, 1831, Limone sul Garda, Italy Died: October 10, 1881, Khartoum, Sudan Church: Roman Catholic Church Beatified: 17 March 1996; Saint Peter's Basilica, Vatican City by Pope John Paul II Ordination: 31 December 1854; by Johann Nepomuk von Tschiderer zu Gleifheim Books: The Writings of St. Daniel Comboni: Correspondence and Reports (1850-1881) of the Founder of the Comboni Missionaries Parents: Domenica comboni, Luigi Comboni.
